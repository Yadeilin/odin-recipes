Cachapas

The Venezuelan Cachapa flavors explode in your mouth

Ingredients

  • A can of sweet corn
  • Corn Fluor, Harina Pan
  • One egg
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat a pan, low heat
  2. Blend the sweet corn for a minute and a half, leave some grains complete, with the egg, sugar and salt
  3. Add some milk and blend 30 seconds more
  4. Pour the mix in a bowl and add some fluor until the mix have more consistency but it is still soft
  5. Pour some mix in the pan forming a circle, as a pancacke
  6. Cook for 2-3 min and flip, cook the same time on the other side
  7. serve with butter and cheese
  8. Enjoy!
