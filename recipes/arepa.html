Arepa

Arepa is the heart of the Venezuelans, eating an arepa will send you right away to Venezuela

Ingredients

  • Precooked corn meal, Harina Pan
  • Water
  • Sal

Directions

  1. Preheat a pan, medium heat
  2. Mix the corn fluor with the water and salt until completely mixed
  3. form a bal with the dough and flatten with your hands
  4. cook during 8 min each side
  5. open in the middle, see picture above, and fill it with anything you desire
Suggestion It can be filled with cheese, meat, ham, chicken, egg, etc
