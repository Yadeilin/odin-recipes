Lasagna

The Italian Lasagna will melt your heart and fulllfil your belly. With this dish your guest will not only enjoy but cherish special moments through this delicious meal

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese

Directions

  1. cook the meat with onions and garlic in a medium heat pan
  2. in a pan boil enough water to deep the pasta
  3. add the tomato sauce to the meat and mix
  4. cook the pasta in the boiling water for 8 min
  5. once all is cooked, in an oven form, lay a layer of pasta folowed by a layer of meat
  6. Add heavy cream, cheese and repeat the previous step
  7. bake around 30 minutes until the cheese is grated
